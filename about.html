Who I Am

I'm Krishna Vamsi Prathipati, a final-year B.Tech student specializing in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at VR Siddhartha Engineering College, Vijayawada. I'm graduating in May 2026 with a CGPA of 8.85.

My primary focus is Computer Vision and Deep Learning — building end-to-end ML pipelines that work in real-world, noisy environments. I published research at IEEE INSPECT 2024 on aircraft detection in SAR imagery, achieving 95.3% accuracy using an enhanced YOLOv8 architecture.

I'm comfortable working across the full ML stack: from dataset preparation and model architecture design, through training and evaluation, to deployment via Flask REST APIs with real-time inference. I also work with LLMs and NLP pipelines using GPT-4 and NLTK.

Outside of engineering, I volunteered with Make A Difference, teaching structured Mathematics lessons to Grade 5 and Grade 7 students in Vijayawada.
