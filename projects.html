Detection of Aircraft from SAR Images Using Deep Neural Networks
Designed an enhanced YOLOv8 architecture augmented with GLCM texture features and CLAHE contrast preprocessing, trained on the TerraSAR-X satellite dataset (844 images). The key innovation was replacing conventional colour-based feature extraction with texture analysis, enabling robust detection against weather interference and dense urban noise — a major challenge in SAR imagery.
